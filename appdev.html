Planning, UI/UX design, development, testing, and maintenance are some of the phases that are involved. Developers create useful and intuitive apps that satisfy certain user or company needs by using programming languages, frameworks, and tools. Depending on the needs of the project, the process might use a variety of approaches, including Agile, Waterfall, or DevOps.
